Plant Disease Detection using Mobile Application

"The Pencil of Equality" is a poignant short film set in a charming middle-class neighborhood, following Amal, a compassionate student, and Rishi, a privileged but disconnected classmate. Amal's humble life and Rishi's wealth converge in a classroom moment when Amal's act of selflessness, breaking his own pencil to share with Rishi, bridges social gaps. As Rishi undergoes a transformative realization about equality in education, the broken pencil becomes a symbol of unity. With a heartfelt score, this film beautifully conveys the profound impact of compassion, understanding, and shared learning in fostering a more harmonious society..
